SHREK

A tribute to the best cartoon movie series out there

Shrek is a 2001 American computer animated comedy film that is known for being iconic. It consists of 4 movies that shaped and made my childhood extremely fun and memorable. With its timeless soundtrack and cinematography, Shrek is a cartoon that is undeniable worthy of this tribute, so let's get into it!

Shrek's most popular characters will be listed below, as well as the 4 movies (Shrek 1, Shrek 2, Shrek 3 and Shrek Forever After). We'll then wrap up this tribute with some fun facts about the cartoon!
